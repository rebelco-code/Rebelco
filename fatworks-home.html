    Frequently Asked Questions

    What makes Fatworks’ tallow different

    Fatworks’ tallow is sourced exclusively from grass-fed and grass-finished animals raised on small family farms. The fat is 100% hormone-free and rendered using a meticulous fine-filtering process that ensures exceptional purity, taste, and quality. This commitment to sourcing and production guarantees a product that’s natural, sustainable, and better for cooking, skincare, or other uses.

    Is tallow shelf-stable, and how should I store it?

    Yes, Fatworks’ tallow is naturally shelf-stable due to its low water content. It can stay fresh for 4–6 months at room temperature and over a year if refrigerated. For long-term storage, refrigeration or freezing is recommended, but it’s not required for daily use.

    How does Fatworks deliver, and how long does it take?

    Fatworks partnered with The Courier Guy to ensure your products are delivered safely and efficiently. Delivery times vary based on location, but most orders are processed and shipped within 2–3 business days. Once shipped, delivery typically takes 1-2 business days for most regions. Tracking information is provided so you can monitor your order every step of the way.

    Why should I replace seed oils with tallow?

    Seed oils, such as canola, soybean, and sunflower oil, are highly processed and often contain harmful trans fats and omega-6 fatty acids, which contribute to inflammation and chronic diseases. Tallow, on the other hand, is a natural, minimally processed fat that is stable at high temperatures, making it ideal for cooking and frying.

    Tallow is free from harmful chemicals and is rich in healthy saturated fats, conjugated linoleic acid (CLA), and fat-soluble vitamins like A, D, E, and K. These nutrients support overall health, hormone balance, and proper cell function. By replacing seed oils with tallow, you’re choosing a healthier, more nutrient-dense option that aligns with traditional cooking practices and real food principles.

    Can beef tallow help with chronic diseases like high blood pressure and diabetes?

    Beef tallow can be a beneficial addition to the diet of individuals with chronic conditions like high blood pressure (BP) and diabetes. Unlike seed oils, which are high in omega-6 fatty acids and contribute to inflammation, tallow is a stable, natural fat that supports healthy cell function and reduces inflammation. It is also free of harmful trans fats and additives, which can worsen chronic conditions.

    Incorporating beef tallow into your diet can improve metabolic health, stabilize blood sugar levels, and promote better heart health by reducing the reliance on inflammatory seed oils.
